Quinoa Cakes recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 cups Quinoa
  • 1 sjallot / small onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 0.5 cups of grated parmesan
  • 0.25 cups parsley
  • 2 tablespoons oregano
  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 4 large eggs
  • olive oil (for frying)
  • salt and pepper
Method:
  1. Cook and cool the quinoa
  2. prepare a dish with parchment paper
  3. Finely chop the sjallot, garlic, herbs and cheese
  4. When quinoa is cool, add the chopped ingredients
  5. add breadcrumbs and salt and pepper and stir
  6. beat the 4 eggs and mix them in
  7. stir untill evenly moistured
  8. pack the mixture tightly in a 1/4 cup measure and place on parchment
  9. panfry the quinoa cakes in olive oil for about 5 mins per side or until golden brown and crispy
  10. let cool and serve
  11. try with avocado on top with some lemon juice!
